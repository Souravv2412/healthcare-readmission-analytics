Test Selection Logic & Data Validity
Normality: Shapiro-Wilk confirmed non-normality in all continuous variables (p ≈ 0 on 5,000 samples) → Mann-Whitney U preferred over t-test. Categorical: Chi-Square used with expected-count verification (0 cells <5). Pre-exclusion: 2,423 rows with expired/hospice discharge codes removed before testing - these patients cannot logically be readmitted. Effect size: Always reported alongside p-value, as large n can make trivial differences significant.
